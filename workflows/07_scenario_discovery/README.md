# Stage 07 — Scenario discovery

## Purpose

Post-process the per-realization metric bank from stage 06 into binary
satisficing labels and gradient-boosted classifiers over drought-feature
space. Cheap to re-run — adding or tuning a satisficing definition does
not require re-running Pywr-DRB. Backs §7.3 main-text and §SI-J.

## Run order

Must run **after** stage 06 (consumes
`outputs/exp09_drb_policy_reeval/<slug>/results/metric_bank.parquet`
and the per-realization drought characteristics from the upstream MOEA
output's `results.json`). All downstream of this stage is figure
production.

## Drivers

| Driver | SLURM | Inputs | Outputs | Working figures |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [satisficing_sweep.py](satisficing_sweep.py)             | (no SLURM; serial)                                                                | metric bank + chars + [satisficing_manifest.yaml](satisficing_manifest.yaml) | `outputs/exp12_satisficing_sweep/<slug>/`        | `outputs/exp12_*/<slug>/figures/` |
| [scenario_discovery_plots.py](scenario_discovery_plots.py) | [slurm/scenario_discovery_plots.slurm](slurm/scenario_discovery_plots.slurm)     | exp09 results dir                                                            | `outputs/exp17_drb_scenario_discovery_plots/`    | `figures/scenario_discovery/`      |

`satisficing_sweep.py` uses `src.satisficing_labels.sweep_manifest` to
fit a GBT classifier per definition in
[satisficing_manifest.yaml](satisficing_manifest.yaml). The manifest
controls the binary label rules (metric, threshold, direction); add new
definitions there without re-running Pywr-DRB.

## Manuscript figures

- Main: Fig 9 (satisficing map; the GBT decision boundaries projected
  to two drought features).
- SI: §SI-J (per-definition manifold figures, classifier diagnostics).

Working PDFs land in `figures/scenario_discovery/` and per-variant
subdirectories. Final assets are regenerated by
`99_manuscript_figures/make_figures.py`.

## SI figure budget

§SI-J targets 2-3 multi-panel figures: classifier ROC summary, GBT
boundary overlays, and per-definition status table.
